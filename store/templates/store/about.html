How We Got Started

Stage Freight was born in the basement of Alex's family home, where a group of friends would jam together after school. What started as an after-school hobby quickly took on a life of its own when they played their first gig at a local open mic night. The name Stage Freight was inspired by the nervous energy they felt before stepping onto that first stage-a feeling they've since learned to channel into their music.

As word spread about their electrifying live shows, the band began to attract a loyal following. Even though the musical influences and personalities of the members are different, the common goal for Stage Freight is to create music that resonates with listeners and inspires them to embark on their own journey.
